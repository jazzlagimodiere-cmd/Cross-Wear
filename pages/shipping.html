Cross Wear Policies

Shipping Policy

Shipping & Delivery

Effective Date: June 24, 2026

At Cross Wear, we take pride in delivering every order with care. Our goal is to ensure your order arrives safely and in excellent condition while providing reliable shipping throughout Canada.

Shipping Locations

We currently ship within Canada. International shipping may become available in the future.

Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 2–5 business days after payment has been received and verified.

During product launches, holidays, or periods of high order volume, processing times may be longer. If significant delays occur, we will notify you by email.

Shipping Methods

Customers may choose from the following shipping options during checkout:

  • Standard Tracked Shipping
  • Express Shipping

Standard Tracked Shipping includes package tracking and is the recommended choice for most orders.

Express Shipping may be available at checkout for an additional cost.

Shipping rates are calculated during checkout based on your delivery address, package size, and selected shipping method.

Packaging

Cross Wear offers two packaging experiences depending on the collection purchased.

Signature Collection

Our Signature Collection is presented in premium gift packaging designed to reflect the quality of the collection. Orders may include a premium presentation box, protective tissue paper, branded seals, and other presentation materials.

Scripture Collection

Our Scripture Collection is packaged securely using protective vacuum-sealed packaging and durable shipping materials to reduce shipping costs while ensuring your garments arrive safely.

Delivery Times

Estimated delivery times begin after your order has been processed and shipped.

Delivery times vary depending on your location, weather conditions, carrier delays, and other circumstances beyond our control.

Tracking Information

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email containing your tracking information, if applicable.

Incorrect Shipping Information

Customers are responsible for providing accurate shipping information at checkout.

Cross Wear is not responsible for delays, additional shipping charges, or lost packages resulting from incorrect or incomplete shipping addresses provided by the customer.

Lost, Stolen, or Delayed Packages

Once an order has been accepted by the shipping carrier, delivery is the responsibility of the carrier.

If your package is lost or significantly delayed, please contact us and we will do our best to assist you in working with the shipping carrier.

Damaged Shipments

If your order arrives damaged, please contact us within 7 days of delivery and include photographs of both the packaging and the damaged product. We will review each case and work with you toward an appropriate resolution.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our Shipping Policy, please contact us.
