Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 24, 2026

At Cross Wear, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

Information We Collect

When you visit our website or place an order, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Order details
  • Device and browser information
  • IP address
  • Website usage information through cookies and similar technologies

Cross Wear does not store your credit card or banking information.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Communicate with you regarding your purchases
  • Respond to customer service inquiries
  • Improve our website and customer experience
  • Detect fraud and maintain website security
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

Payment Processing

Payments made through Cross Wear are securely processed by trusted third-party payment providers, including Stripe and PayPal.

Cross Wear does not collect or store your complete payment card information. Payment information is handled directly by the payment provider you choose and is subject to their respective privacy and security policies.

Order Fulfillment

To manufacture and fulfill your order, we may share the information necessary to complete your purchase with trusted business partners, including Gabe Clothing.

This information may include your name, shipping address, contact information, and the products you ordered. Our fulfillment partners may only use this information to manufacture and deliver your order.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve website performance
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Help prevent fraud and unauthorized activity

You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some features of the website may not function properly.

Sharing Your Information

Cross Wear does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We only share information when necessary to:

  • Process your payments
  • Manufacture and fulfill your order
  • Ship your products
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our customers, business, and website from fraud or misuse

International Processing

Some of our service providers, including payment processors, may process or store personal information outside of Canada. As a result, your information may be subject to the laws of other jurisdictions.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

While no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, we strive to protect your information using industry-standard security practices.

Your Rights

Depending on applicable Canadian privacy laws, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information where legally permitted
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Cross Wear may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated Effective Date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us.
